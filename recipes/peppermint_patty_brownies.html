Peppermint Patty Brownies

What are Peppermint Patty Brownies?

Peppermint Patty Brownies are a festive and flavorful twist on classic brownies, combining rich chocolate batter with layers of gooey peppermint candies baked right in. Each bite delivers a moist, fudgy texture with refreshing mint accents—an ideal dessert for holidays, dinner parties, or anytime you want an extra special treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups margarine (or butter), melted
  • 3 cups white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 5 eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 24 small peppermint patties (e.g., York Peppermint Patties), unwrapped

Step-By-Step Guide

  1. Preheat and Prepare Pan

    Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 13×9-inch baking pan so the brownies release easily after baking.

  2. Combine Wet Ingredients

    In a large mixing bowl, combine the melted margarine (or butter), white sugar, and vanilla extract. Stir until smooth. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition until fully incorporated.

  3. Add Dry Ingredients

    Into the wet mixture, stir in the all-purpose flour, unsweetened cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt. Mix until the batter is smooth and evenly blended.

  4. Reserve Some Batter

    Set aside approximately 2 cups of the brownie batter in a separate bowl. You’ll use it later to cover the peppermint patties.

  5. Layer Brownie Batter

    Spread the remaining batter evenly into the bottom of the prepared baking pan.

  6. Add Peppermint Patties

    Unwrap each peppermint patty and arrange them in a single layer over the batter, spacing them about ½-inch apart.

  7. Cover with Remaining Batter

    Carefully spread the reserved batter over the layer of peppermint patties so they are covered./p>

  8. Bake

    Bake in the preheated oven for 50 to 55 minutes, or until the edges begin to pull away from the sides of the pan and a toothpick inserted into the brownie layer comes out clean or with moist crumbs—not raw batter.

  9. Cool and Serve

    Allow the brownies to cool completely in the pan on a wire rack. Once cooled, cut into squares and serve. The cooling time allows the brownies to set and the peppermint patties to meld into the chocolate.
